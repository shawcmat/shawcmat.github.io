Who am I?


I am a geospatial data analyst, developer, and researcher, passionate about solving real world problems using creative technological solutions. Holding a master's degree in Geography, I have expertise in GIS, data science, environmental remote sensing, and spatial statistics.


Flexibility and focus.


The landscape of data science is always changing, making flexible adaptation to new technologies an important aspect of professional work. Having a fundamental knowledge of how data is structured allows for quick transitions between technology stacks. Some of the technologies I have experience with include:

  • Python, including packages such as NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-Learn, Pysal, and rasterio.
  • R, including libraries such SF, Raster, Terra, and Tidyverse.
  • Web development, including HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
  • ESRI ArcGIS products, including ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS online, as well as ArcPy, and the ArcGIS javascript API.
  • Open-source GIS standard frameworks, including GEOS, GDAL, and PROJ.
